What we Offer

Nestled in the heart of Puglia, Badia San Michele offers a serene and natural escape. Our family-run agricamper provides a unique and authentic experience, complete with free-roaming rabbits. Come relax and unwind in our peaceful haven!

Our place in brief.

Why not come visit us?

Our place is a magnificent retreat immersed in nature. Surrounded by the breathtaking seas of Salento, it offers a serene and picturesque environment that allows you to truly connect with the natural world

We provide all the amenities necessary for a comfortable stay, including hot showers without the need for tokens, convenient trash bins, and a place to refill water. Additionally, we offer a selection of groceries such as wine, oil, and eggs to ensure you have everything you need for a perfect vacation.
